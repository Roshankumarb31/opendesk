# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all.

- Be respectful and inclusive
- Assume good intent, communicate clearly
- No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Keep discussions constructive and focused on the project

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainers privately. Actions may include warnings or removal from the community spaces.

Let’s build something great together.
